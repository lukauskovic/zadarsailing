Frequently Asked Questions

To save some time for all of us, read this section. Less talking, more sailing!

What is a private tour?

A private tour means that only your group and the skipper will be aboard.

What if a tour in NOT private?

A tour is not private in case you book the 40€/person option. It means we can fill the rest of the ship's capacity with other people/groups.

Do I need to have any sailing experience?

Of course you don't, we'll show you everything you want to know.

Can we arrange a different departure time?

We can, just let us know on time!

What should I bring with me?

You can bring your swimsuit, a towel, a hat, sunscreen, bottle(s) of water, some snacks and cash in case you want to stop for lunch.

Can we stop for lunch/swimming?

Of course we can, we'd be delighted to!
